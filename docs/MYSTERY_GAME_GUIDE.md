- They get a packet with their character name
- Open Envelope 1 â†’ "Oh, I'm the [Log Lady/Detective/etc.]! I have this secret..."

- Everyone cheers, toasts, takes photos

**Bottom Line:** They have fun talking, eating, and piecing together a story. No pressure.
