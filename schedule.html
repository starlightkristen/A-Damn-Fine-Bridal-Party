Make sure you have all of these ready before the event:

  • Physical clues in sealed envelopes
  • Kitchen torch for cupcake reveal ceremony
  • Mystery playlist (Twin Peaks soundtrack)
  • Props: magnifying glasses, fake evidence, "confidential" folders
  • Prize for the winner (Twin Peaks themed gift)
  • Camera or phone for group photos
  • Backup activities if time permits

üîÑ Backup Plans

Prepared alternatives if things don't go exactly as planned:

If Guests Are Stuck:

Have pre-written "anonymous tips" ready to drop if no one is making progress after 15-20 minutes of investigation.

If Guests Aren't Participating:

Have a co-host or planted enthusiastic guest to help draw others into the mystery.

If Running Long:

Skip or shorten traditional shower activities at the beginning. The mystery is the main event!

If Running Short:

Have extra games ready (Twin Peaks trivia, "How Well Do You Know Marlena?") or extend the investigation phase.

‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Warning - Cupcake Torch Reveal:

Using a kitchen torch requires proper safety precautions:

  • Practice the technique beforehand in a safe environment
  • Keep torch away from decorations, curtains, and flammable materials
  • Have a fire extinguisher or damp towel nearby
  • Consider alternative methods: heat gun, hot spoon, or pre-made reveal cards
  • Ensure adequate ventilation
  • Never let guests handle the torch
